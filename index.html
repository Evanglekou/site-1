Koukoutzeli HerdCare

To ensure the highest quality of animal production and streamline B2B distribution, FARMA KOUKOUTZELI P.C. has developed a proprietary Android application.

The app acts as a digital hub for farm managers, veterinarians, and dairy distributors, allowing real-time monitoring of herd health and milk yield logistics.

  • IoT integration for real-time cattle health monitoring.
  • Daily dairy yield tracking and quality analytics.
  • Supply chain logistics for B2B dairy distributors.
  • Digital veterinary logs and automated feed schedules.
