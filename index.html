The Entity

01

Operating under the official registration of EVANGELIA MPELEKOU and doing business globally as STUDIO EBITA, our enterprise sets a new standard in the Retail Trade sector.

We are categorized as Miscellaneous Retailers, meticulously curating collections that span Sporting Goods, Hobby essentials, Musical Instruments, and Books. Our mission is to elevate everyday retail into an aesthetic and culturally enriching experience.

Studio Ebita Mobile Hub

To enhance our physical boutique experience, EVANGELIA MPELEKOU (Studio Ebita) maintains a proprietary e-commerce application.

The app functions as a digital concierge. Customers can browse our exclusive inventory, manage their loyalty memberships, and scan in-store items to uncover the rich history and craftsmanship behind every product.

  • In-Store Barcode Scanner & Audio Previews
